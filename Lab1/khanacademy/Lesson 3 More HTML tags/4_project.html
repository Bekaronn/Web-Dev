Pasta Carbonara

  • Time: 30 minute
  • Serves: 10 minute
Ingredients Quantity
pasta 1
tomato 2
milk 300ml

Step 1: boil the pasta

Step 2: cut and fry tomatoes

Step 3: add pasta add spices

Step 4: add milk and wait 3 minute

Pizza

  • Time: 45 minute
  • Serves: 10 minute
Ingredients Quantity
puff pastry 1
tomato 2
sausage 1
tomato pasta 1
cheese 200g

Step 1: roll out the dough and place it on a baking sheet

Step 2: spread with sauce

Step 3: put sausage, tomatoes

Step 4: add cheese on top and place in oven for 40 min
